How CleanSL Works

A simple, transparent process designed to improve waste collection accountability.

Submit & Track

Residents report waste collection issues or track scheduled pickups directly through the platform.

Verify & Process

The system verifies submissions, applies AI checks where applicable, and assigns tasks to authorities.

Resolve & Notify

Authorities resolve the issue, update the status, and residents receive confirmation notifications.
